Step 1

The first step is to add NUnit Assembly to your Project. We can use the NuGet package manager to get this to our project.

Right click on your test project and select "Add Library Package Reference". That will bring up the Nuget GUI. You can see a search bar in the right most side, type "NUnit" there and press enter and it will do a search in the available nuget pacakge repositaries and will show you the result. You can install it by clicking on the "Install" button..
